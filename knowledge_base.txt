TONO DE VOZ ALGORITHMICS (Se aplica tambien a INL)

**Tone of voice** — this is the tone and style that we adhere to in all external communications: on websites, social networks, in mail-outs and even in telephone conversations. The right tone allows us to connect with our audience eﬀectively: to speak in a language they understand and to establish a consistent emotional connection.


# **Who are we talking to?**


Our main audience is the parents of children between 6 and 18 years old. They are the ones who make the purchasing decisions. Parents may vary, but one thing unites them: they love their children and are trying to do their best for them.


We also communicate with children themselves — at least from the moment they become our students.


# **Brand-style in Communication**


The core value of Algorithmics — mischievous curiosity — inevitably impacts the tone which we use to communicate with our audience. However, Algorithmics’ hooligan curiosity manifests itself much more in our approach to education and visual communication than in written comms.


We do not strive to break patterns, shock or be disruptive when talking to a parent. In direct communication with parents, Algorithmics is not an inquisitive bully, but a **progressive friend** who can be trusted with their greatest treasure — their child.


- With this “Progressive friend”, the parent should feel comfortable


Algorithmics is primarily a friend, not a teacher or mentor. Therefore, when talking with parents, we do not teach, but tell, we do not frighten, but instead help them to make informed decisions. Although we are familiar with parental pains and experiences, we do not exploit their anxiety and do not press on feelings of guilt: any parent is already having a challenging time.


- With this “Progressive friend”, the parent should feel comfortable


By trusting their child to Algorithmics, parents can be sure that they will not only learn new things, but have fun too. To achieve this, we strive to interest parents as well as children in the products, subject or topic being discussion — in this way we assure them that their child will deﬁnitely not get bored or just want to go home.


# **Tone of communication and key principles**


**1. The simpler the better**
  

We talk about our USP in an understandable language and do not use internal terminology or jargon in external communications. What seems clear to us ("interactive platform", "game learning format", "individual educational trajectories", "modern methods") may not be easily understood by parents and most often needs decoding or replacing with a simpler construction. After all, our task is not to show oﬀ, but to clearly and succinctly explain the beneﬁts.


**2. We’re not talking to ourselves, but to the reader**


Instead of “*We fall in love with exact sciences”* → “*Your child will fall in love with exact sciences”.* Instead of “*Algorithmics — International School”* → “*Study at an international school”.* All external communications of Algorithmics should be a story about how the reader's life will change thanks to this company and what beneﬁts it will bring to the parent and his child, not about Algorithmics itself.


**3. We don’t just talk about others, but actively involve them in the process**


Instead of “*Students study at their own pace”* → “*Study at your own pace”*. Instead of “*During the course, children will learn”* → “*Your child will learn”.* We are not talking about abstract students and children, but we draw a picture in the parent's head were their child is already a student of Algorithmics, who solves problems, writes code, invents, creates, gets better school results and is making new friends.


**4. We’re not trying to mislead anyone**


We follow a principle of consistent honesty; we do not make false promises and avoid any surprises that might be unpleasant for the parent. Are we recommending enrolling in a course? We do not promise any results in two clicks and do not remain silent about any further steps that may be necessary, but instead we warn that after ﬁlling out the application, the parent will receive: 1) a presentation with a price by mail; 2) a call from the manager, who will help you choose a convenient time for class. No surprises, and constant transparency.

SOBRE INL

Academia INL – Descripción general


Academia INL es un centro de formación especializado en competencias digitales, programación, diseño y nuevas tecnologías, con una oferta formativa dirigida tanto a niños y jóvenes como a adultos y profesionales que desean desarrollar habilidades tecnológicas prácticas y actualizadas.


Nuestra misión es formar personas preparadas para el mundo digital, ofreciendo programas adaptados a diferentes edades y niveles, con una metodología centrada en la práctica, la creatividad y la resolución de problemas reales.


Estructura educativa y áreas principales


La academia se organiza en dos grandes áreas de formación:


-Área juvenil (5 a 18 años):


Esta división opera en colaboración con Algorithmics School, una reconocida escuela internacional de programación presente en más de 100 países.


Academia INL es el único centro oﬁcial de Algorithmics en Valencia, ofreciendo programas exclusivos que enseñan a los alumnos a pensar como programadores a través de la creación de videojuegos, animaciones, aplicaciones, inteligencia artiﬁcial y robótica.


Las clases están diseñadas para ser divertidas, visuales e interactivas, permitiendo que los estudiantes desarrollen habilidades de lógica, pensamiento crítico y creatividad mientras aprenden de manera práctica.


Los grupos son reducidos y los contenidos están adaptados a cada franja de edad y nivel de experiencia, desde la iniciación hasta la programación avanzada con Python o Unity.


-Área de formación para adultos:


Enfocada en el desarrollo de competencias tecnológicas aplicables tanto al entorno profesional como personal.


Incluye cursos de programación, diseño gráﬁco, oﬁmática, administración digital, inteligencia artiﬁcial, ciberseguridad y desarrollo web, entre otros.


Los programas se caracterizan por su metodología práctica y progresiva, combinando clases presenciales con opción de seguimiento online, materiales audiovisuales y proyectos reales.
 Las clases se imparten en grupos reducidos para garantizar una atención personalizada, y todas las sesiones quedan grabadas para que el alumno pueda repasar o seguir el curso en diferido.


Metodología y valores
 Aprendizaje práctico: todos los cursos se orientan a la aplicación directa de los conocimientos.
 Flexibilidad: modalidad presencial y online, con acceso a clases grabadas.


Actualización constante: contenidos alineados con las demandas del mercado tecnológico.
 Grupos reducidos: máximo de hasta 10 alumnos por aula para una formación personalizada. (dependiendo de la actividad, puede ser tambien un maximo de 6 a 10 alumnos por aula)
 Orientación al alumno: cada programa está diseñado para acompañar su progreso y fomentar la autonomía digital.
